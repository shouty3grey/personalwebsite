EchoTap - Vibration

As a culmination of ES50(Introduction to Electrical Engineering course), my group and I prototyped a device that outputs vibrational patterns depending upon the characteristics of input sounds, to assist hard-of-hearing users with audio detection. With an Arduino Nano ESP32, we developed the circuitry using concepts accumulated from the year.
